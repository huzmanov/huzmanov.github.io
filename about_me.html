About me (IN PROGRESS)

In 2023, my career trajectory took a significant turn as I embraced the role of Project Engineer Intern at New Line Structures. This marked my inaugural step into the construction management industry, where I eagerly immersed myself in a transformative learning experience. Tasked with contributing to the construction of a $40 million hospital building in Queens, New York, I embarked on a journey filled with hands-on challenges and invaluable insights. Collaborating closely with seasoned professionals, I honed my skills in project management, scheduling coordination, and quality assurance, laying a solid foundation for my future endeavors in the dynamic world of construction.

During my college years, I immersed myself in biochemistry and neuroscience studies while taking on leadership roles. As Vice President of the Neuroscience Club for two years, I organized events and discussions. Prior to that, I served as Alumni Coordinator, fostering connections between past and present students. These experiences not only enriched my academic journey but also honed my leadership and networking skills.
